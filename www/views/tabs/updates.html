Feats, Scrolls, Gathering & a Web App

July 2026
  • • Feats — take a feat instead of ability points at level-up (Tough, War Caster, Elemental Adept, and the stat feats)
  • • Spell scrolls — read a scroll to cast its spell in or out of combat (still costs mana, no preparation needed)
  • • Gathering & lockpicking — mining and fishing nodes and locked chests, worked with the right tool and a skill check, with tiered loot
  • • New MILL sign-in — one modal for every Nostr login (extension, Amber, remote bunker, private key, or a brand-new identity); all signing stays in your browser
  • • Installable as an app — add Pubkey Quest to your home screen, and links now unfurl with a preview card
  • • Mobile polish and bug fixes ahead of open testing

The Adventure Comes Alive

June 2026
  • • Quests, dungeons & encounters are playable — pick up quests from NPCs and boards, explore points of interest as branching node-walks, and hit random and hand-authored encounters while travelling
  • • Rotating daily quests handed out by innkeepers
  • • Spellcasting is real — cast spells in and out of combat with mana and material components, and drink potions or throw items mid-fight
  • • Combat depth — class abilities (Rage, Second Wind, Ki…), conditions (poisoned, prone, frightened…), and saving throws
  • • Character progression — level up from XP, spend ability points, and grow your HP and mana
  • • Building interiors — enter inns and taverns, rent a room, and sleep the night
